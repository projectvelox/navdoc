About Us

NavDoc is developed by the collaborative efforts of the students and alumni of the college of computer studies Programmers' Guild which was established last 2016. The mission of the guild was to create solutions for the innovative world and to enhance the developer's skill in collaborating work with others as well as the exchange of ideas. The guild is situated in Jaro, Iloilo City in the Mary Thomas building inside the Central Philippine University Campus.

NavDoc was made to aid prospect patients having trouble finding the perfect doctor for them. Depending on their location and symptoms or even based on the specialization there is in need. it also has an easy appointment access, payment and statistics for data analytics and a feedback for the doctor to know his patient's response.


Features


Convenient Appointment

One tap and an appointment will be booked for you. Just a few clicks and a doctor will be suggested to you. And when It's all done, payment is completely seamless thus, a digital receipt will be emailed to you instantly.

Google Maps

Going to a doctor? Not knowing who or where to get a consultation? We will help you find a perfect doctor that best suites you based on your specialty and current location with various travel modes: walking , biking , private cars and transits with its ETAs per travel mode. A selection of routes will be suggested.

Health Chat Bot

Chiron our very own health bot. Chiron's name was derived from the Greek centaur Chiron, known for his knowledge and skill in healing. It will help you find the doctor that best suites you, by telling them your symptoms and a suggestion will be produced by Chiron.

Mobile Responsive

Many online users is via mobile devices. Mobile users are more likely to use sites that are designed for mobile use instead of those that simply display a desktop version on mobile devices. Who knows what the future technology innovation holds? A responsive design will accomodate the current needs of the user as well as the future.
